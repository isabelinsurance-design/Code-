🔌 Connect real data & your CRM

The Tempo Agent (agent/tempo_agent.py) runs on the tracked computer and records the active app, window title, URL, idle time and screenshots, then POSTs them to the Tempo Server (server/tempo_server.py), which classifies and stores them. This dashboard reads the aggregated report from GET /api/activity — so it shows live data automatically when served by the server. To push time entries into your CRM per client/contact, forward the same samples to your CRM's API. Edit categories.json to tune what counts as productive vs distracting.
